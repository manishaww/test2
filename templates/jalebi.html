Jalebi

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon besan (gram flour)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ghee
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix all-purpose flour, besan, yogurt, and baking soda. Add water to make a thick batter.
  2. Keep the batter aside for 12 hours to ferment.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan and add the fermented batter in a jalebi maker or piping bag.
  4. Squeeze the batter in hot oil in a spiral shape and fry until golden brown.
  5. In a separate pan, heat sugar, water, and cardamom powder to make a syrup.
  6. Soak the fried jalebis in the syrup for a few minutes and serve hot.
