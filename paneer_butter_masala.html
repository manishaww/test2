Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  3. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add paneer cubes and garam masala. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Stir in cream and simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
